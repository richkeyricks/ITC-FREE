Intelligence FAQ

How does ITC ensure zero-latency execution?

Zero-latency is achieved through our dedicated "Titan Bridge" infrastructure. This custom-built middleware, developed in Rust, establishes a direct-memory communication line with the MT5 terminal process. By bypassing standard graphical processing and utilizing asynchronous I/O operations, we reduce the command-path latency to sub-millisecond levels. Furthermore, our servers are co-located in Equinix data centers (LD4/NY4), providing the absolute minimum physical distance to broker execution gateways.

Is my proprietary strategy logic protected?

Absolutely. Your competitive edge is your most valuable asset. ITC Terminal functions as an execution and orchestration layer. All proprietary trading logic, indicator parameters, and entry criteria remain strictly local to your environment or your private VPS. Haineo AI Systems does not have access to your individual strategy code, ensuring that your "Alpha" stays completely sovereign and secure within your authenticated instances.

What is "Stealth Mode" and why is it critical for SEO and trading?

In institutional trading, broadcasting your Stop Loss (SL) and Take Profit (TP) levels to a broker's server can expose your positions to "liquidity hunting" or manipulation during low-liquidity windows. ITC's Stealth Mode keeps these levels hidden internally. Our local execution agent monitors the price feed in real-time and only executes a market order at your target price, effectively cloaking your risk parameters from the rest of the market participants and predatory algorithms.

Can I manage institutional capital with Infinite Copytrade?

Yes. The Infinite Copytrade module is specifically designed for capital allocators and fund managers who need to broadcast trades from a central "Master" node to a vast decentralized network of "Slave" accounts. The system automates equity proportioning and risk-percentage scaling, ensuring that every account, regardless of size, maintains identical percentage exposure. Our heart-beat monitoring protocol ensures 99.9% synchronization uptime across the entire network.

What are the system requirements for Haineo AI Terminal v2.0?

For optimal performance, we recommend running the terminal on a Windows-based VPS with at least 4 vCPUs and 8GB of RAM. High-speed fiber connectivity with a latency of less than 10ms to LD4 or NY4 hubs is highly recommended for high-frequency scalping strategies. The Haineo AI Infrastructure is also compatible with modern web browsers for remote monitoring and basic management through our secure WebMonitor portal.
