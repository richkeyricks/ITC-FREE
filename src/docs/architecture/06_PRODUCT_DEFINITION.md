## 1. The Misconception
Many users confuse **"Signals"** (Telegram Channels) with **"Strategies"** (ITC Logic).
*   **Signal (Raw Material):** "Buy Gold Now @ 2000". This is just information. It can be good or bad.
*   **Strategy (The Factory):** *HOW* to process that signal to make money safely.

## 2. The "Strategy Preset" (.json)
In the Gravity Marketplace, a **Strategy** is a **Configuration File** that completely automates the ITC software. When a user buys a Strategy, they are buying the **Setups & Rules** of a Pro Trader.
